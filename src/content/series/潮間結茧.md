description: "「潮間結茧」

本次駐留通過與當地漁民的合作，將廢棄的捕鱼工具轉化為記憶的容器。在其中心，一枚缠繞着旧網、歷经风霜的浮標，喚起了海洋與陸地之間脆弱而坚韌的紐帶。一旁，由村民制作的定置網模型復甦了傳統捕撈的幽微智慧。這些作品將勞作、歷史與想象編織在一起，顯影出一種與海洋共生的语言。

「Cocoon Between the Tides」

This residency transforms abandoned fishing tools into vessels of memory through collaboration with local fishermen. At its center, a weathered buoy entwined with old nets evokes the fragile yet resilient bond between sea and land. Alongside, a fixed-net model crafted by villagers revives the subtle ingenuity of traditional fishing. These works weave together labor, history, and imagination, revealing a shared language of endurance and coexistence with the ocean."
