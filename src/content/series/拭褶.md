description: "「拭褶」是2021至2023年間，於迁徙與漂泊中沉澱的圖像。牠們如陰影里浮动的錨， 微弱而固執地維繫着這幾年的軌跡。牠們關乎相遇、離散、尋覓、漫遊……也悄然連接着晝與夜的邊緣、光與影的縫隙。

「Goodbye... Ripples」 is the images settled from migration and wandering between 2021 and 2023. Like anchors floating in the shadows, they weakly yet stubbornly keep the track of these years. They are about meetings, partings, searchings, and wanderings... and quietly connect the edges of day and night, the cracks of light and shade."
