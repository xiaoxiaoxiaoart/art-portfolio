  「躲藏在你的陰影里」收錄了2024至2025年間的圖像記錄，彼時，我回到廣東，落腳廣州。南方的湿熱重新將我包裹，為避暑熱，我時常躲在你的陰影里，縫隙間温吞微凉的灰瀰漫，搅渾了非黑即白的時刻。
  
  'Hiding in Your Shadow' collects the image records between 2024 and 2025. At that time, I returned to Guangdong and settled in Guangzhou. The damp heat of the south wrapped around me again. To escape the summer heat, I often hid in your shadow, where the lukewarm, cool gray in the cracks spread out, blurring the black-and-white moments.
