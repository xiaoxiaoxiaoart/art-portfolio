'Scratch' collects the image traces from 2019 to 2020. I tried to make the paint friction, overlap, and dissolve on the surface, stripping them of concrete narratives and forms. These interwoven marks faintly preserve the unspeakable memories of that period.

Series of 10 prints, 2019 - 2020"
