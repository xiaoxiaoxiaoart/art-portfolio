title: "囊 Pouch"
description: |
  「囊」將廢棄的工業模具、蓄水容器與耐火砖組合，築起層層缠繞的圍合空間。當這些器物被剝離了原本的實用功能，牠們便轉化為一種留存記憶的容器，固執地封存着這片場域的工業殘跡與潛在敘事。
  
  裝置扎根於樓梯下方一處低矮、封閉的儲藏室——這里曾是堆放廢料的消隱之所。通過對物質的重新編碼，作品在此構建出一座空間檔案館。材料的拼貼與重組激活了場域沉睡的記憶，使那些殘留的物件轉化為喚醒知覺的媒介。觀者步入這處被遺忘的角落，與工厂過往的餘音不期而遇，並在空間、物性與人的彼此缠繞間展開凝視。
  
  「Pouch」 assembles discarded industrial molds, storage vessels, and refractory bricks into layered enclosures. Stripped of utility, these forms act as containers of memory, stubbornly preserving the site’s industrial traces and latent narratives.
  
  The installation is rooted in a low, enclosed storage room beneath the staircase—a former place of disappearance for waste. By recoding matter, the work builds a spatial archive here. The collage and reassembly of materials activate the site’s sleeping memory, turning those residual objects into media that awaken perception. Visitors step into this forgotten corner to encounter the echo of the factory’s past, and to gaze within the entanglement of space, object, and human presence.
  
  Installation, 2025
  Industrial resin molds, Industrial lamp, refractory bricks, etc.
